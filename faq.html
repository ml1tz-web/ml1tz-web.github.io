Frequently Asked Questions

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?
To print a document, connect to the HZ network and use the "Print" option in your document software. Select the HZ printer and send your document.
How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?
Use the scanner available in the library. Select "Scan to Email" and enter your email address to receive the scanned document.
How can I buy something on the HZ web shop?
Visit the HZ web shop, log in with your student account, select the items you want, and proceed to checkout.
How can you book a project space?
Use the HZ booking system online to reserve a project space. Log in with your student credentials and select an available slot.
What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?
Make sure you have a valid parking permit. Enter the parking lot with your permit displayed and follow the signs to park in designated areas.
